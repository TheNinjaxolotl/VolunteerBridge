How it works

Simple for everyone

Whether you're a student collecting hours or an organization finding dependable help — we've made it easy.

01
Create your profile

Sign up free with your school email. List your interests, skills, and availability. Takes under 3 minutes.

02
Browse & apply

Search by category, date, or distance. Apply to opportunities with one click — no lengthy forms.

03
Log hours & get verified

After each shift, your supervisor approves your hours digitally. Download your verified certificate anytime for college apps.

01
Register your organization

Create a free nonprofit or business account. Verify your status — we welcome nonprofits, schools, hospitals, and mission-driven companies.

02
Post volunteer roles

Describe the opportunity, hours needed, and any requirements. Listings go live instantly and reach thousands of motivated students.

03
Manage & verify

Review applicant profiles, communicate through the platform, and digitally sign off on hours — all in one dashboard.
